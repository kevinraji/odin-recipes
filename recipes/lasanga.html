Lasanga Recipes

Description

Per Serving: 643 calories; protein 41.3g; carbohydrates 53.4g; fat 29.3g; cholesterol 108.3mg; sodium 707.3mg. Full Nutrition

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce

Steps

  1. Step 1 Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. Step 4 Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
